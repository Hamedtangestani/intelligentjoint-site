Intelligent Joint (IJ) is an advanced structural framework that leverages AI to create robotic designs that are both highly realistic and fully functional for simulation and real world deployment.

Each IJ acts as a modular building block available in both physical and virtual forms that seamlessly integrates with other components to form complete, dexterous robotic systems.

  • Scalable & Flexible Adaptable to various robotic applications.
  • Rapid Assembly, Fast prototyping and easy implementation.
  • Customizable & Intelligent Supports intuitive control and tailored configurations.
